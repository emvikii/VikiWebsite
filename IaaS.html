IaaS

What is Infrastructure-as-a-Service?

IaaS, or infrastructure as a service, is a type of cloud computing that offers clients basic computation, network, and storage capabilities on demand, via the internet, and on a pay-as-you-go basis.

IaaS allows you to grow your compute capability while spending less on IT. In the past, businesses owned and cared for their own computing equipment in a data center on-site. However, this frequently needed a significant initial investment to manage sporadic spikes in activity.
